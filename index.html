What is “HAJIMI”？

Introduction

Hajimi (also written as “Hakimi” or “Hajimi”) is an internet meme. It did not originally refer to cats but came from the anime Uma Musume: Pretty Derby. In edited fan videos, a line spoken by the character Tokai Teio was remixed and sounded like “Hajimi.”
Because the pronunciation was exaggerated and humorous, it was widely shared and imitated online, eventually becoming a popular meme.

History of Hajimi

1. Origin

“Hajimi” originally came from the anime Uma Musume: Pretty Derby. In fan-edited videos, a line spoken by the character Tokai Teio was remixed and sounded like “Hajimi.” Because the pronunciation sounded exaggerated and humorous, it quickly attracted attention online.

2. Spread

Around 2023, the phrase became widely shared online.

  • Remix music
  • Edited videos
  • Internet memes

3. Meme Culture

Over time, “Hajimi” developed into a recognizable internet meme. It is often used in humorous videos and memes and has become an example of how remix culture and online communities create and spread new trends.
